// overview

Overview

PromptPacks is a corrugated cardboard box manufacturer based in Puthenchira, Thrissur, Kerala — established in 2016. I designed and built their complete business website to establish a professional online presence and reach B2B customers across India.

The site covers their full product offering: custom corrugated boxes with flexo and offset printing, with MOQ 1000 and 3–4 day turnaround for pan-India delivery.
